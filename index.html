10

What Blue May Rightly Worry About

A senior strategy response to the most reasonable commercial and brand concerns.

Will audiences feel deceived?

The persona should be introduced with a clear trust architecture, ensuring legitimacy rather than ambiguity.

Is this too risky for an insurance brand?

The rollout should separate trust-building lifestyle content from more direct planning relevance, with clear governance at each stage.

Could this become gimmicky or cringe?

Only if the persona is designed as spectacle. Our recommendation is a mature, socially believable, life-stage-relevant woman.

What if growth comes without trust?

Success should be measured not only by followers, but by saves, shares, return attention, sentiment, and audience quality.
