01

The Opportunity

Insurance communication often underperforms not because the category lacks importance, but because the communication model feels too product-led, too corporate, and too socially detached.

Key Observation

For modern 35+ audiences, trust is built through relevance before conversion.

Blue’s opportunity is not only to explain products more clearly. It is to create a social communication model where savings and protection feel naturally connected to real life stages, priorities, and aspirations.

That means moving away from communication that feels overly corporate or transactional, and toward a persona-led system that is more socially credible, more human, and more platform-appropriate.

03

Why This Makes Sense for Blue

This is not about making insurance trendy. It is about making the brand more understandable, more relatable, and more appropriate to the platform.

Blue’s category challenge is not only awareness — it is relevance, trust, and how financial products are introduced in social environments.

By shifting from a product-first communication style to a persona-led social model, Blue can position savings and protection as part of a broader conversation around planning well, living responsibly, and preparing for the future.

This is especially important for a 35+ audience, where major decisions are shaped less by urgency and more by maturity, trust, and the desire to make thoughtful long-term choices.

Key Strategic Message

This is not a virtual influencer gimmick. It is a communication model.

The role of the persona is to build trust, relevance, and social credibility first. Product connection comes later, once the audience has context, familiarity, and reason to care. The objective is not to create a fictional celebrity; it is to create a persuasive communication system that makes Blue more platform-appropriate and more memorable over time.

Why This Can Win Across Instagram and Threads

The communication model works because each platform performs a different strategic role.

Platform Role

Instagram

Main visual and storytelling platform. This is where persona identity, content world, aspiration, and planning logic become visible and emotionally resonant.

Platform Role

Threads

Lighter conversational layer for relevance, tone of voice, and point of view. Threads supports familiarity, recency, and more everyday social credibility around the persona.
